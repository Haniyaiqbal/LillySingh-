Lilly Singh

Lilly Saini Singh (born September 26, 1988) is a Canadian YouTuber and former talk show host, who originally appeared under the pseudonym Superwoman, her longtime YouTube username. Born and raised in Scarborough, Toronto, Ontario, Singh began making YouTube videos in 2010.
